The Power of Parallel Processing

Traditional document extraction processes documents sequentially, working through one section at a time. Extractor AI takes a fundamentally different approach with its advanced parallel processing architecture.

Our system automatically divides documents into logical sections and processes them simultaneously across multiple processing units. This parallel approach dramatically reduces processing time, especially for large or complex documents.

How It Works

  1. Document Analysis: The system analyzes the document structure to identify logical sections
  2. Intelligent Chunking: The document is divided into optimal chunks for parallel processing
  3. Distributed Processing: Each chunk is processed simultaneously by dedicated processing units
  4. Context Preservation: Cross-references and relationships between sections are maintained
  5. Result Merging: The processed chunks are intelligently merged into a unified output
  6. Quality Assurance: The final result is verified for consistency and accuracy

Technical Architecture

  • Distributed Computing: Processing is distributed across multiple computing resources
