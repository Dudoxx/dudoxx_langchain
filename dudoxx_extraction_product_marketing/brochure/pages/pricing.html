Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a page?

A page is defined as a standard 8.5" x 11" or A4 page or equivalent. For non-standard documents, we count approximately 3,000 characters as one page.

Can I change plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with up to 100 pages of processing. No credit card required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire transfers for Enterprise plans.
