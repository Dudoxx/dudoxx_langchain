The Extraction Process

Extractor AI transforms complex documents into structured data through a sophisticated multi-stage process:

  1. Document Input: Upload or paste text from any supported format
  2. Domain Identification: Automatic detection of document type and domain
  3. Intelligent Extraction: Domain-specific processing to identify key information
  4. Normalization: Standardization of extracted data for consistency
  5. Structured Output: Delivery of clean, organized data in your preferred format
